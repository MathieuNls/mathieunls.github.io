CESI.EXIA (French Computer Engineering School), Aix-En-Provence, France

Ms.C Student • Sep, 2010 — Dec, 2013. GPA: 3.8/4

Under the supervision of Alexandre Sbriglio I worked on DarwinIT : Problem resolution inspired by the Nature. «The survival of the fittest theory» by Darwin says that the continuous process of natural evolution by the survival of most hardly, of the most adapted, without discontinuity in the time, increase the chances of survival in a relatively stable environment. Could we duplicate this kind of processes, used by the nature since millenniums, to build better algorithms which would be capable of evolving, of improving, to be more successful in their environments ?
